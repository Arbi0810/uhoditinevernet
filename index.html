How to get rid of cravings for sweets

How to get rid of cravings for sweets There are several ways to get rid of the habit of overeating. One of them is the transfer of taste and emotional sensations from a favorite delicacy to a not very favorite, but at the same time harmless to the figure. So, you can transfer the feelings of taste and desire for a treat that you like to eat to the not very favorite, but at the same time very tasty. It just takes a little time and effort. Let's look at the methods of transfer.The way we eat unconsciously creates an impression of a desire for a treat. This subconsciously brings about the occurrence of a certain taste preference. The perception of taste is affected by the brain. The brain places food in front of your eyes in the form of a "star". The "star" appears and is remembered in the body. If there is no such preference, the "star" does not taste good. Therefore, you need to try to bring your favorite into your mind in the first place. The preferred method is a familiar food. It makes the taste of food and the will to eat it is restored. This method is good because it is familiar, familiar, but it is also low in calories.
